Nature’s Body

X



Nature is always dynamic. It has given life to different forms and created its own balance.The energy and diversity it has within itself, which provides this cycle, grows from the soil to the earth.While we are a tiny part of this nature, contrary to what we think, our body is not our only strength.The balance within ourselves comes out of our body and integrates with the nature we live in. It is our spine that keeps us upright; The fact that it is very strong but also spinnable symbolizes that we have a symbiotic relationship with nature and that we can evolve.The membrane in the continuation of the brain, to which the spinal cord is surrounded, tells about our development from the roots in nature and our close bond with nature.Each vertebra represents the power of nature.Solar energy, living things, vegetation, harmony, borders, parts, control mechanism, recycling… When we come together with all these occurrences and do not try to dominate as an outside influence, but establish a real connection and integrate, we become a useful and real living thing that can stand upright.To not be stuck inside our body, but to overflow, to understand where we came from, is to finally find ourselves and exist.
